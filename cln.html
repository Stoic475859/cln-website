About CLN

CLN is one of the Philippines most iconic fashion brands, known for elegant footwear, premium bags, modern apparel, and timeless accessories crafted with sophistication.

History of CLN

CLN began as a small leather boutique dedicated to handmade bags and footwear.

Through the years, the brand expanded into malls across the Philippines.

Today, CLN is a leading fashion powerhouse known for modern, classy essentials.
